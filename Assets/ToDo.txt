Gameplay:
Torches that can light bombs.
Destructable crates.
Destructable objects of different materials that are impervious to some damage but susceptible to others. (Metal doors, metal crates)
Bombs can destroy cannons.
Water buckets that can be picked up and can douse bombs and cannons and torches.
SpikeWalls to place enemies

GUI:
Achievement Screen.
Fun Speech bubbles for characters (like a thought in that moment in time)

Aesthetics:
Varieties of contstruction blocks

Meta:
Achievement for beating the game without getting any enemies killed(Pacifist).
Achievement for beating the game without letting any enemies live(Blood Thristy).
Achievement for beating the game without letting any friendlies die(Savior).

3D Assets:
Broken Wooden Crate
